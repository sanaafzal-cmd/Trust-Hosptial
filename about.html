About Us

We Aim to Provide Medical And Health Facilities to the Poor and to the Needy

“Save Humanity And whoever saves the life of one person, it is as if he has saved the whole of mankind. {Al-Quran: 5-Surat Al-Maidah - Ayat 32}” –

Haq Bahoo Trust aims to build a hospital providing all kinds of health facilities to the poor and to the needy. The Hospital is aimed to be built in a rural area, highly lacking health facilities, surrounded by the people with poor health conditions and high disease rates needing great health care facilities and health awairness. Haq Bahoo Trust aims not only to enhance the health conditions of the people in the area but also the people that visit the Hospital in search of better health facilities and treatments from the areas in the surroundings.
